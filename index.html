Hello!

I'm Blake, and after spending 15 years in Automotive Manufacturing, I decided I wanted to make a change. I've always wanted to be involved in tech, I was actually supposed to go to college for Computer Science in 2007. Unfortunately that was the same time that the market was crashing, and financailly I couldn't make it happen.

Here I am, 15 years later, teaching myself how to be a front end web developer. Thank goodness for all the amazing resources available to those who are willing to learn. FreeCodeCamp is where I got my start, and I highly encourage you to do the same if you're interested in taking this path.
